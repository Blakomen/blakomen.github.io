About Me

Hello! I'm Henry. I've worked in tech for over a decade on a wide range of things from projects in a services company (Provoke) to a small UK startup (Tailster.com) to big tech (Facebook / Meta).

I started my career as a software developer and moved into managing engineering teams, and I'm passionate about empowering people with technology.

I live in London with my partner and our two pets (a cat from Taiwan and a trailhound from Dogs Trust), and he and I still play too much League of Legends (a habit I picked up from my time living in Taipei). I also enjoy discovering new restaurants, hiking and well designed metro maps.
